Rendang Recipes

Rendang is a spicy meat dish originating from Indonesia. It is slow-cooked in coconut milk and a mixture of lemongrass, galangal, turmeric, ginger, and chilies.

This dish is often served at ceremonial occasions to honor guests and is considered a national dish of Indonesia.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef, cut into cubes
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp chili paste
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan over medium heat and add the coconut milk.
  2. Add garlic, onions, chili paste, turmeric, and ginger. Stir well.
  3. Add the beef cubes and lemongrass. Stir to coat the beef with the spices.
  4. Simmer on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the beef is tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt to taste and serve with steamed rice.
