Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken

This simple, sensational marinade and sauce for grilled chicken is made with lemon, garlic, rosemary, and butter. Separate the marinade into thirds: 1/3 for marinating, 1/3 for basting, and 1/3 for topping.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Divide the marinade into three parts: one for marinating, one for basting, and one for serving.
  3. Marinate the chicken in 1/3 of the marinade for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  6. Baste with the second portion of marinade while grilling.
  7. Serve with the reserved marinade on top.
  8. Enjoy your delicious rosemary lemon grilled chicken!
