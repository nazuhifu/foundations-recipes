Nasi Goreng Recipes

Nasi Goreng is a popular Indonesian fried rice dish that is flavorful and easy to make. It's often served with a fried egg on top and can include various proteins and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, corn, bell peppers)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrot and mixed vegetables, and cook until tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble until cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce. Stir well to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
